# Qbservable.GroupBy\<TSource, TKey, TElement\> Method (IQbservable\<TSource\>, Expression\<Func\<TSource, TKey\>\>, Expression\<Func\<TSource, TElement\>\>, IEqualityComparer\<TKey\>)
