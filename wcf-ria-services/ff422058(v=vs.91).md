## Remarks

Once a reference is established, referenced [DomainContext](ff422732\(v=vs.91\).md) instances will be consulted when resolving the [EntitySet](ff423164\(v=vs.91\).md) for an [Entity](ff422907\(v=vs.91\).md) type.
