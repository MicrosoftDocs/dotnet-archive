## Remarks

You apply the DeleteAttribute attribute to a method to indicate that the method is a delete method. If your method matches the expected method signature for a delete method, you do not have to apply the DeleteAttribute. The RIA Services framework will infer that a method with the expected delete signature should be treated as a delete operation. You apply the [IgnoreAttribute](ff423126\(v=vs.91\).md) to a method to prevent the RIA Services framework from generating a domain operation for that method.
